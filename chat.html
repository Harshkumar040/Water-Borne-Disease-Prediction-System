Disease Statistics

Common Pathogens

Frequently Asked Questions
What are the first signs of waterborne diseases?
Common early symptoms include diarrhea, vomiting, abdominal cramps, fever, and dehydration. Seek medical attention if symptoms persist beyond 24 hours.
How does pH affect drinking water safety?
pH below 6.5 causes pipe corrosion releasing metals like lead. pH above 8.5 reduces disinfection efficiency. Ideal range: 6.5–8.5.
What temperature is dangerous for water storage?
25°C–40°C promotes rapid bacterial growth. Store water below 20°C when possible.
Best home water purification methods?
Boiling (most effective), activated carbon filters, UV purification, ceramic filters, and chlorine tablets. Combine boiling with filtration for maximum safety.
