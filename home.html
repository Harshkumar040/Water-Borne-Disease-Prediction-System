About Our Prediction System

AquaRisk combines environmental science with artificial intelligence to provide accessible water disease risk assessment. The platform interprets three fundamental parameters: pH, turbidity, and temperature — each critical to microbial survival and chemical contamination. Our model is trained on historical data from CDC, WHO, and national water quality databases, ensuring high precision for diverse regions.

pH Influence: Acidic waters corrode pipes releasing heavy metals; alkaline waters reduce disinfection efficiency.
Turbidity Impact: High turbidity shields bacteria from UV/chlorine, raising gastroenteritis risk.
Temperature Effect: Warm water accelerates pathogen replication — critical for cholera outbreaks.
